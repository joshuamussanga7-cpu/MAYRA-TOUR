Our Philosophy

The Mayra Story

Mayra Tours was born out of a passion for Tanzania’s raw beauty and a commitment to professional excellence. Based in Arusha, we specialize in luxury safaris and Zanzibar escapes that prioritize your comfort, safety, and health.

Our unique approach combines the thrill of the wild with medical-grade safety standards, ensuring that every journey is as secure as it is breathtaking. We don't just show you Tanzania; we ensure you experience it with absolute peace of mind.

The Mayra Advantage

Health-First Professional Travel

At Mayra Tours, we integrate professional health-care standards into every expedition. Our deep background in nursing ensures that your safety is monitored with medical precision, especially during high-altitude Kilimanjaro treks.

  • Medical-Grade Kits: Advanced first-aid equipment on every vehicle and trek.
  • Health Monitoring: Daily oxygen and heart rate checks for mountain climbers.
  • Nursing Expertise: Staff trained to recognize and manage altitude and travel-related health issues.
  • Amref Flying Doctors: Comprehensive emergency evacuation cover for all guests.
